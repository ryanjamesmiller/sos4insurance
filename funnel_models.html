Pipeline Power System — Three Visual Models

Three distinct approaches to visualizing the four components. Review and choose one to integrate into the website.

Version 01
The Layered Funnel

A classic Simon Bowen-style model. The funnel shows the prospect journey from targeting to close. The Force Multiplier wraps the entire system as an outer accelerant ring — communicating that AI amplifies every stage, not just one.

Version 02
The Pipeline Engine

A horizontal engine model. The prospect enters on the left as a raw lead and exits on the right as a qualified appointment. Each component is a stage in the engine. The Force Multiplier sits beneath as the fuel line powering every stage — a visual metaphor for AI as infrastructure, not a feature.

Version 03
The Bullseye Model

A Simon Bowen-style concentric ring model. The Production Floor sits at the center — the outcome everything is built around. Each ring is a layer of the system that surrounds and drives toward it. The Force Multiplier is the outermost ring, amplifying everything inside it. This model communicates: every component exists to protect and produce the result at the center.

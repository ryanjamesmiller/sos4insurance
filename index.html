Supporting Framework

The Foundation

Most performance problems are not process problems — they are identity problems wearing a tactical disguise. The Foundation is the personal operating system built into every SOS engagement to address the work beneath the work. Built on two interlocking frameworks — the Wound Analysis Framework (WAF) and the Grounded Alignment Framework (GAF) — it surfaces the root causes of underperformance and gives every producer a daily operating rhythm to close the gap between who they are and how they show up. Grounded in identity. Authentic under pressure. Purposeful in pursuit. Because systems fail when the person running them is operating from the wrong foundation.

What We Build

Seven Deliverables. You Own All of Them.

SOS Consulting is not generic sales training. Every deliverable is co-built with you — not delivered to you — so it actually gets used, actually fits your business, and actually transfers to your team.

01

Sales Playbook

Your full documented sales system: messaging, outreach sequences, discovery frameworks, objection handling, and closing language.

02

ICP Definition

A written, specific description of your ideal client — who they are, what they believe, what they are fighting, and what disqualifies them.

03

The 1440 Prospecting Sequence

14 touches over 40 days across 6 channels. Installed and running on qualified prospects before the engagement closes.

04

Pipeline Tracker

A CRM setup or structured tracker that gives you visibility into pipeline stage, velocity, and rep activity.

05

Onboarding SOPs

Step-by-step documentation for onboarding new producers so the system is teachable, repeatable, and not dependent on tribal knowledge.

06

Team Scorecard

A weekly accountability tool that measures leading indicators — calls, appointments, follow-ups — so performance issues are caught early.

07

Identity & Mindset Work

Because systems fail when the person running them is operating from the wrong foundation. Every engagement includes structured identity and mindset work to align who your team is with how they show up.

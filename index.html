The Pipeline Power System

Four Components. One System. Built for Your Agency.

SOS Insurance is a done-with-you engagement. We build it alongside you and your team — then hand it to your producers and make sure they can run it without you.

Component 01

The Target Lock

We define your ideal account with precision — the size, the industry, the pain profile, the fit signals, and the decision makers worth your team's time. Clarity before pursuit, every time. When this is built correctly, your producers stop chasing everything and start winning the accounts your agency is actually built to serve.

Component 02

The 1440

A 14-touch, 40-day, 6-channel prospecting sequence built specifically for insurance producers pursuing employer groups and commercial accounts. Every touch is intentional. Every channel is deliberate. Every message delivers value before it makes an ask. Your producers run it. You don't have to manage it.

Component 03

The Value Cadence System

Getting the meeting is only half the battle. The VCS is the engagement framework that keeps your producers present and visible from first conversation to closed deal. It eliminates the silence that kills momentum and ensures that when a prospect is ready to move, your agency is the one they call.

Component 04

The Force Multiplier

We show your producers exactly how to use AI to run the entire system faster, sharper, and with less friction — so they spend more time building trust and closing business, and less time on research, prep, and repetition. This is not about replacing the relational nature of insurance sales. It's about removing the guesswork.

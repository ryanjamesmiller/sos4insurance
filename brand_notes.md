# SOS Brand Guidelines — Key Findings

## Logo
- Primary logo: SOS wordmark + "Sales Operating System" subtitle — blue (#4682B4) on white
- Logo mark: SOS wordmark only (larger, bolder)
- Tagline version: SOS mark + "Structure | Optimize | Scale"
- Dark background version: white SOS mark on graphite (#41424C)
- The "O" in SOS contains a distinctive upward chevron/arrow icon inside it
- Minimum digital size: 100px wide (full logo), 35px wide (icon only)
- DO NOT: stretch, apply shadows, rotate, use stroke, change icon position

## Color System
### Primary
- Steel Blue: #4682B4 (RGB 70, 130, 180) — main brand color
- Graphite: #41424C (RGB 65, 66, 76) — dark text/backgrounds

### Secondary
- Mist Gray: #F5F7F8 (RGB 245, 247, 248) — light backgrounds
- Dusty Blue: #3E5C76 (RGB 62, 92, 118) — darker blue accent

### Accent
- Warm Sand: #CBBFAD (RGB 203, 191, 173) — warm neutral accent

## Typography
- Font family: DM Sans (all weights)
- Headings: DM Sans Bold
- Sub-headings: DM Sans SemiBold
- Body text: DM Sans Regular
- Available weights: Thin, ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold, Black

## Logo on Backgrounds (approved combos)
- Blue SOS on white ✓
- White SOS on Steel Blue (#4682B4) ✓
- White SOS on Graphite (#41424C) ✓
- White SOS on Dusty Blue (#3E5C76) ✓
- White SOS on Warm Sand (#CBBFAD) ✓
